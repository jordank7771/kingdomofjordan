I Ain't Try To

I Ain't Try To - Throwaway Album Cover for MoBandz

In attending Purdue University, I met and befriended MoBandz, an aspiring rap artist. Briefly working with him and his collective, Swim Industries, I was commissioned to make an album cover for his upcoming single, I Ain't Try To. While listening to sample snippets, the sound made reminded me of the concept of zero gravity.
Although this cover design was not ultimately to be used with the final publishing, this cover is my most polished work. Since I had more experience in using the Adobe Creative Suite, more subtle features were added to make this design feel more immersive in its appearance.

never enough

never enough - Sample Album Cover

In seeking to use my image manipulation skills as a form of monetary gain, I wanted to create sample covers that best showcase my ability to create clean and comprehensive designs. All of the elements used in this design were royalty free images, or otherwise, personally photographed on my phone.

This was the first cover that I have utilized Photoshop and Illustrator, two applications that are a part of the Adobe Creative Suite. A lot of the color grading properties were a challenge to understand at first, but through taking this sample design through multiple iterations, the final design displays a warm feeling that is similar to my experiences watching the sun set.

Finna Get Popped

Finna Get Popped - Published Album Cover for H.E.R.U

In 2019, I was commissioned to create an album cover for my long-time friend, H.E.R.U (formerly known as IcedOutMK), who was just starting his career as a performing artist. Finna Get Popped is still considered to be one of my best productions prior to college and is responsible for me continuing to be commissioned to create covers for other artists.

This composition consists of exclusively royalty-free images that were further edited through a wide range of free and open source applications, such as GIMP and Inkscape.
