About Me

Allow Me to Introduce Myself

Jordan King

Jordan King is a college student at Purdue University studying Game Development. He is currently employed at Purdue Athletics where he is an in-venue graphics producer, responsible for maintaining video board graphics as well as assisting with live and in-game sports broadcasts.

Although he is heavily interested in video games, he also interacts with music and other various forms of artistic expression. As a content creator, animator, album cover artist, and trained in musical arts, he displays a wide range of technical and design knowledge.

Jordan is the oldest in a family of five, hailing from the Atlanta area. Though he spends a lot of time in front of a screen, he enjoys an active lifestyle and and takes pride in his active engagement in his community.
